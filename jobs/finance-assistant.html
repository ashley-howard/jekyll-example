Finance Assistant

Details

Based in London

Up to £26,544 - £30,618 a year - Temporary

About

An exciting opportunity for an accurate and capable finance professional to join the team of a successful eCommerce business, a leader in the independent nursery sector.

The Finance Assistant will work closely with Director and our purchasing team to ensure accurate data input and to resolve all queries in a timely manner. They will have the use of some of the most sophisticated data entry software on the market to assist in the role.

Natural Baby Shower which was started from the “kitchen table” in 2004, now has a team of 60+ dedicated professionals working across its spacious Bracknell-based office building with on-site warehouse, and large retail store nearby. The company which has a strong eco-ethos has an impressive growth curve with ambitious plans and offers excellent training and progression for all team members.

Main Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Inputting purchase invoices and daily bank payments
  • Liaising with suppliers to resolve queries and discrepancies on purchases
  • Bank reconciliations
  • Reconciling purchase ledger accounts to supplier statements
  • VAT Reconciliations
  • Payroll processing
  • Experience

Must haves:

  • Experience of Accounts Payable work
  • AAT qualified or training preferred
  • Good IT and Excel skills
  • The Ideal Candidate will have previous experience in accounting and data entry, preferably within an eCommerce business.

Schedule:

  • Monday to Friday

Work remotely due to COVID-19.
